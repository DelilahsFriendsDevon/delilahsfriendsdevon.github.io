About

Hi, I’m Izzy. I offer caring, dependable dog (or cat!) walking for busy owners. Your pet gets exercise, attention, and lots of love while you’re away.
The company name sake is that of the sweet dog of my partner and I. Delilah is a 4 year old beagle cross lurcher, who sparked my passion for dog walking.

Outside of dog walking, I've spent a few years volunteering at an alpaca farm as simply an excuse to help out and be around animals. I also work in sports technology with a background in physics. It's not just dogs I love, I have some stranger pets - a frog, lizard, tortoise, shrimp, snails and fish! And my partner and I are hoping to welcome home a cat soon. I have experience walking dogs from BorrowMyDoggy and many of friends and family. I'm now looking to take on some regular clients!

How It Works

Drop them off with me at Mill Gardens, EX15 1ZL or I can pick them up from you for free within Collumpton!
Extra charges apply for travel outside of Collumpton at £0.50 per mile for a pickup and drop off.
